# In a meeting room

I am in a meeting room with a modern teleconferencing setup. I have my laptop and my phone too.

I connect The Fly to my phone via Bluetooth, join the meeting with my phone. I also join with my laptop. But I set The Fly to mute and my laptop also on mute, because I am using the teleconference desk microphone arrays to talk

The single audio stream from Bluetooth is being recorded to the microSD card. The Fly's audio output (probably earbuds) is not in my ear

# Joining a meeting remotely

I connect The Fly to my phone and join the meeting.

If The Fly is built with a WM8960 then I will be listening through the earbuds and talking to the inline mic of the earbuds.

There is a mute button available.

If I am using the M5Stack's built-in mic, I will need to use the push-to-talk function

The microSD card is recording two interleaved streams.
