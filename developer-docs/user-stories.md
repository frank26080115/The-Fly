# In a meeting room

I am in a meeting room with a modern teleconferencing setup. I have my laptop and my phone with me.

I connect The Fly to my phone via Bluetooth, join the meeting with my phone. I also join with my laptop. But I set The Fly to mute and my laptop also on mute, because I am using the teleconference desk microphone arrays to talk.

The single audio stream from Bluetooth is being recorded to the microSD card.

The Fly's audio output is muted. The Fly's microphone(s) are muted.

# Joining a meeting remotely

I connect The Fly to my phone and join the meeting.

If The Fly is built with a external I2S codec then I will be listening through the earbuds and talking to the inline mic of the earbuds.

There is a mute button available, for both the mic and the earbuds.

The microSD card is recording two interleaved streams. One channel contains sound from my local mic, the other channel contains data from the remote callers.
